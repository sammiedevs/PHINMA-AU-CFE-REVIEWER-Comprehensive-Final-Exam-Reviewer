Guidelines for Using the Exam Generator

The Exam Generator on our website allows users to create customized exams based on their notes. Please follow the steps below to ensure a smooth experience:


1. Accessing the Exam Generator

- Click the "Open Generator Exam" button to launch the exam generator website in a new window.

- This will redirect you to the external platform that generates exam questions based on the provided text.

2. Preparing Your Notes

- Before opening the generator, we recommend clicking the "Open Notes" button.

- This will give you access to your saved notes, allowing you to copy the relevant content.

3. Generating the Exam

- Copy the desired notes content from the notes section.

- Paste the copied text into the exam generator website in the appropriate field.

- Follow the instructions on the generator website to create your exam.

4. Finalizing Your Exam

- Review the generated exam to ensure accuracy and relevance.

- Save or print the exam as needed for study or assessment purposes.
